Making some simple tweaks to your application can result in significant cost savings. In this blog post I’ll cover both server-side and client-side strategies you can adopt to reduce your monthly geocoding bill. Rate Limiting Rate limiting is the server-side strategy we use to protect our customers from excessive billing, while additionally shielding users from degraded quality-of-service.
